# Market Share & Competitive Benchmarking – Telecom Industry

## Objective
Analyze and compare telecom operators' market share using interactive Power BI dashboards.

## Key Metrics
- Market Share %
- Subscriber Growth
- ARPU
- Regional Performance

## Visuals Used
- Ribbon Chart – Market leadership movement
- Funnel Chart – Customer conversion
- Map – Regional performance
- KPI Cards – Key indicators

## Tools
- Power BI
- DAX
