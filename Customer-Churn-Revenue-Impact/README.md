# Customer Churn & Revenue Impact Analysis

## Objective
To analyze customer churn patterns and evaluate their impact on revenue using SQL and Power BI.

## Business Questions
- Which customers are most likely to churn?
- What factors contribute to customer churn?
- How much revenue is impacted by churn?
- Which customer segments have the highest churn risk?

## Tools Used
- MySQL
- Power BI
- DAX

## Project Structure
- SQL: Data extraction, transformation, and churn analysis
- Power BI: Interactive dashboards and business insights
